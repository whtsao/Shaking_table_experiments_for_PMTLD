The Excel file contains raw voltage data from sensors, with a sampling frequency of 100 Hz.

Sensors and conversion factors:

Voltage  = Displacement of the displacement sensor (pendulum platform displacement):  1 V = 25 mm
Voltage_0 = Shaking table displacement (ground displacement):                  1 V = 25 mm
Voltage_1 = Shaking table acceleration (ground acceleration):                  1 V = 0.50412 g
Voltage_2 = Load cell:                                          1 V = 10 kg
Voltage_3 = Tank acceleration:                                  1 V = 0.49438 g
Voltage_4 = Pendulum platform acceleration:                       1 V = 0.50073 g
